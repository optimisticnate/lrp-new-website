## ðŸŽ¯ **SEO STRATEGY FOR EACH NEW PAGE**

### Required Elements:
1. **Schema.org Structured Data** - Service, LocalBusiness, Offer
2. **SEO Metadata** - Title (60 chars), Description (155 chars), Keywords, Canonical
3. **H1 with Location Keywords** - "Service Name + Lake of the Ozarks"
4. **Hero Section** - Icon, Headline, Subheadline, Pricing, CTA
5. **Why Choose Us** - 3 benefit cards with icons
6. **Service Breakdown** - 4-6 services with CheckCircle icons
7. **How It Works** - 4-step process with numbered circles
8. **FAQ Section** - 5-6 questions optimized for voice search/AIO
9. **Testimonials** - 2 customer testimonials with 5-star ratings
10. **Final CTA** - Phone (573) 206-9499 + Book button
11. **Internal Links** - Link to related services, fleet pages, partners
12. **External Links** - Link to relevant Lake Ozarks attractions/venues

### Keyword Strategy:
- **Primary Keyword** - Service name + "Lake of the Ozarks" or "Lake Ozarks"
- **Secondary Keywords** - Service variations, related terms
- **Long-tail Keywords** - Question-based (who, what, when, where, why, how)
- **Location Keywords** - Osage Beach, Lake Ozark, Camdenton, Bagnell Dam
- **Modifier Keywords** - Professional, luxury, reliable, affordable, safe

### Content Length Target:
- **Minimum**: 1,500 words
- **Optimal**: 2,000-2,500 words
- **Maximum**: 3,000 words

### Image Optimization:
- Use descriptive alt text with keywords
- Compress images for page speed
- Use WebP format when possible
